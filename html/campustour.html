Campus & Facilities
LHS is proud to be an International Baccalaureate (IB) world school catering to students from the ages of 3 to 15 in the Primary Years (PY), Middle Years (MY) and the High School (HS).
At LHS, we are dedicated to our Mission Statement – “To provide stimulating, engaging academics integrated with enhanced opportunities for technological innovation, sports and the arts.” As you visit our campus and website, you will see that our learning community is dedicated to the development of knowledgeable, caring, confident and responsible global citizens. Parents describe our learning community as “warm and welcoming, where every child is cared for and valued” – which is further evidence that our Mission is alive and thriving.
