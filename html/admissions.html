The admissions team is excited to partner with families as they consider their educational choices for their children. As we take you through our admissions process, we want you to know that our primary goal is to ensure your child’s success at LHS. Our admissions process looks at the character of a child academically and socially to make sure CIS is the right fit for her or him. We welcome diversity and hope families stay with us for their child’s entire academic journey.
VISION
The Lourdes High School community strives to nurture, challenge, and empower learners who impact a remarkable and sustainable future.
MISSION
Canadian International School places students at the centre of learning, creating opportunities to be reflective, adaptive, and inclusive that leads them to become socially responsible and dedicated voices for positive change.
DEFINITION OF LEARNING
Learning at LHS is a transformative process that leads to conceptual understanding, demonstration of competencies through application, and character development as seen through the LHS Core Values.
To make our language of learning accessible to everyone, we connect through learning, Conceptual Understanding, Competency and Character, with three simple sentence stems, one for each.

Secretary’s message
Lourdes High School Nandini Layout is a part of ‘The Cambridge group of Institution’.the school is founded by the visionary Dr. Louisa Manohari. This institution was established in the year 2002 with the Motto ‘Knowledge is power’ it is guided by the able leadership of Maria Sunder the school Secretary.
The school has demonstrated tremendous growth, commitment to quality education for overall development of students right from Nursery..
The school supports the individual well being of the student and provides a school environment that allows every student to thrive as they work towards achieving their personal best performance.
We at Lourdes spare no efforts to make the educational experience of our student meaningful and relevant for the present time, equipping each individual to face the challenges. The school will be committed to the pursuit of academic excellence and values based development for all students in our care. The school shall promote an ethos which seeks to cultivate and nurture each student with love for all human beings. Today we stand proud as an institution and have watched batch after batch portal and become discerning citizens.
Our focus is not only academic excellence alone but rather on creating sound value system. Our teaching faculty and support staffs are the edifice of this institution. Under the guidance of this efficient team our students are blossoming into individuals who are to become positive contributors to the society.
MARIA SUNDER M - Secretary
